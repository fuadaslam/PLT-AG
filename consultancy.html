Agro Advisory Excellence

Your directory to the world of Horticulture, starting from A, all the way to Z. Professional guidance for sustainable and profitable farming.

Why Our Agro Advisory?

The primary sector is the foundation for any other business sector to be built on and flourish. This is why farmers are regarded as one of the most integral and priced possessions of every economy. But are they being valued and rewarded for their sweat?

They are not much acquainted with the revolutions in agro-technology and thus pay the price of being misfed and misled by wrong guidances. This mainly occurs because they are unfortunate to trust what the sale-driven market feeds them.

What farmers require is guidance from authentic sources who are not driven by greed but for the welfare of the farmer population. With proper assistance and guidance, they should get accustomed to the habit of distinguishing what's genuine and what's toxic or unfit.

What PLT-AG Provides

PLANT LABZ TECH and its team has witnessed and studied over the years how this chain of exploitation progresses. We believe in bringing in solutions with innovation.

Our intention is entrusted in keen monitoring of the agricultural market and come up with the right and guaranteed schemes and solutions that can help farmers produce a yield that is of high quality, using both organic and inorganic products as needed.

We guarantee a society that can trust what is being served on their plates.

What Agro Advisory Has For You

  • Analyzing your agricultural plot and determining the possible quality it possesses. Each farmland has varying properties and so does the location. Both these play a pivotal role in determining several factors that necessitate a good quality yield.
  • Studying the possibilities of different crop patterns to be practiced on your land. Determining if the land is more likely to suit cash crops, value-added crops or if it requires mixed cultivation.
  • We also undertake the distribution of both organic and inorganic products that facilitate soil management, regulate the availability of nutrients in the soil and control the budding and spreading of various plant-based diseases.
  • We try and educate talented and potential minds on various streams that handle quality management of the yield. We also make sure to spread awareness on good plant health and ways to promote, regulate and maintain them.
  • Routine monitoring of the cultivation process and procedures to guide farmers and spread information and guidance to everyone working in the sector.

We are conscious and aware of the services we provide and their importance in the field of agriculture. Our charges for this consultancy and guidance shall be dealt directly with the landowners. Through our services, we intend to transform our agriculture sector to a level that will benefit and reward the ones behind it fairly.
