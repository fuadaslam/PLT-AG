Why our Agro Advisory?

The primary sector is the foundation for any other business sector to built on and flourish. This is why farmers are regarded as one of the most integral and priced possession of every economy. But are they being valued and rewarded for their sweat? They are not much acquainted with the revolutions in agro-technology and thus pay the price of being misfed and misled by the wrong guidances.

This mainly occurs because they are unfortunate to trust what the sale-driven market feeds them. They are misguided on details of Organic farming and production. What farmers require is guidance from authentic sources who are not driven by greed but for the welfare of the farmer population. With proper assistance and guidance, they should get accustomed to the habit of distinguishing what’s genuine and what’s toxic or unfit. This will not only reap quality yield but also guarantee profit for the farmer community.

What PLT-AG provide?

PLANT LABZ TECH and its team has witnessed and studied over the years how this chain of exploitation progress. We believe in bringing in solutions with innovation. Our intention is entrusted in keen monitoring of the organic agro market and come up with the right and guaranteed schemes and solutions that can help farmers produce a yield that is toxic-free and fully original organic produce. We guarantee a society that can trust what is being served on their plates.

What Agro Advisory has for you?

  • Analyzing your agricultural plot and determining the possible quality it possesses. Each farmland has varying properties and so does the location. Both these play a pivotal role in determining several factors that necessitate a good quality organic yield. The average rainfall the area receives, availability of water, temperature, texture, and type of soil, the occurrence of required minerals in the soil, etc are majors factors that contribute to a good yield.
  • Studying the possibilities of different crop patterns to be practised on your land. Determining if the land is more likely to suit cash crops, value-added crops or if it requires mixed cultivation, etc. This is important to ensure that the farmer gets the best variety yield from their plot than wasting the resources on the wrong crop.
  • We also undertake the distribution of purely organic products that facilitates soil management, regulate the availability of nutrients in the soil and controls the budding and spreading of various plant-based diseases.
  • We try and educate talented and potential minds on various streams that handle quality management of the yield. We also make sure to spread awareness on good plant health, ways to promote, regulate and maintain them. To ensure that the yield is non-toxic, the crop must stay away from any sorts of abiotic or biotic stress factors.
  • Routine monitoring of the cultivation process and procedures to guide farmers and spread information and guidance to everyone working in the sector.
We are conscious and aware of the services we provide and it’s importance in the field of agriculture. Our charges for this consultancy and guidance shall be dealt directly with the landowners. Through our services, we intend to transform our agriculture sector to a level that will benefit and reward the ones behind it fairly.
