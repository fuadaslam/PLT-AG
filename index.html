ABOUT US

PLT AG is an initiative in organic agricultural commodity production that vows to sustain the ecosystem and people. We put forth a viable alternative production method for farmers, that procures better harvest with PLT AG commodities. As an innovative team of youngsters, we work to develop Integrated agricultural strategies for total well-being.

Our Services

Integrated Fertilizer Nutrient Management

This is a mechanism undertaken to ensure the proposed rate of productivity by optimizing all methods of benefits which includes inorganic, organic, alongside other biotic and biological components in integration. This is performed by maintaining the fertility of soil as well as external plant nutrient support.

Integrated Pest Control Management

It is an ecosystem-based integrated methodology and approach developed with an aim of focusing on preventing the occurrence of damages posed by pests. This is adopted and performed by integrating multiple strategies of resistance methods, preparing biological controls, manipulating habitat, and modifying few cultural practices.

Integrated Disease Control Management

The unexpected failure of crops is one of the grave dangers farmers face. This system of management aims to adopt a varied range of practices and measures that will help control, preventing and curing plant diseases. This is undertaken with great care to meet the possibilities of reducing such diseases among crops by controlling the responsible pathogens.

Farm Development

This ensures agricultural development in the farming units by improving the overall condition that makes and arranges for best potential results and reaps in farms. This includes providing the right knowledge, assistance, and guidance, along with push factors of technology that will accelerate the need for appropriate inputs for quality outputs.

Higher sustainability

Sustainability is an ideology that has been globally discussed and popularized and with the increasing trend of the global population scale sustainability is of crucial importance. Sustainability seeks healthy usage of natural and biotic resources by taking into consideration that the future generation to have access to these resources.

For the consumer

The benefits and goodness of adopting healthy agricultural practices, quality soil treatment, productive output management, and organic farming are not exclusive to the farmers or primary sector workers. The whole idea of organic farming and quality yield is to get non-toxic food served on the plates of every consumer as a whole.
