About

PLT AG is an initiative in organic agricultural commodity production that vows to sustain the ecosystem and people. We put forth a viable alternative production method for farmers, that procures better harvest with PLT AG commodities. As an innovative team of youngsters, we work to develop Integrated agricultural strategies for total well-being.

We intend to bolster agricultural performance in the state of Kerala through newly tested methods. We maintain a unique board of experts on the agrarian procedures to strengthen our primary sectors. Profound research and plenty of support from the team help farmers to understand the right mechanism for the quantity and quality of products along with their real market values.
